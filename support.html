Frequently Asked Questions

What does Ninja Learning teach?

Ninja Learning focuses on math — arithmetic, fractions, algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics — through bite-sized lessons and quizzes.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link shortly.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your progress is tied to your account, so you can pick up right where you left off.
